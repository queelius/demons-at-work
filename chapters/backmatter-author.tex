\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{About the Author}

Alex Towell writes science fiction and horror about people doing difficult work inside systems that do not deserve them. He holds two master's degrees (Computer Science and Mathematics) from Southern Illinois University Edwardsville, where he is completing a PhD in Computer Science.

He has filled in many forms.

He lives in Illinois.
